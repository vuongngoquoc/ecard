Because as a {'cf_site_title'} member you can quickly get reminders of your friends' and family's birthdays, anniversaries. You will never forget a birthday again. Membership includes the following exclusive benefits:

Existing Members
How do I change my email address?
You can change your email address by doing the following:
  • Click here to sign in, enter your user name id and password.
  • Click Member tool My Account link
  • On your 'My Account' page find text box User email address and enter your new email address.
  • Our system will auto save your new email to database right after you enter new email and click outside the text box
How do I change my password?
You can change your password by doing the following:
  • Click here to sign in, enter your user name id and your current password.
  • Click Member tool My Account link
  • On your 'My Account' page find and click button Change Password
I have lost my password. What can I do?
You can get your password by doing the following:
  • Click here to go to sign in page
  • On page 'Login' look for icon
  • Enter your email to the text box and click Send Password button
How do I cancel my {'cf_site_title'} membership?
You can cancel your membership by doing the following:
  • Click here to sign in, enter your user name id and password.
  • Click Member tool My Account link
  • On your 'My Account' page, under tab Financial Information find and click button Delete Account
  • If your account is a Free Basic Membership, then system will delete your account right away
  • If your account is a Paid Account, then you have to wait for admin to receive your request. Admin will cancel your subscription and he/she might down grade your Paid Account to Free Basic Membership. When your account becomes a Free Basic Membership, you can delete it
Greeting cards
I cannot view the eCard
Many of our interactive greetings use an enhanced animation called Flash. To view these greetings, you will need to have a Flash plug-in. Please make sure you have a current version of 8.0 or higher.

You can download the Adobe Flash Player for FREE by clicking here.

After installation is complete, please go to the link provided in your original email. The greeting will automatically play from this point.

Some other ecards used java applet. You might need java plug-in as well. Click here to download Java software.

How do I send a greeting card?
Below is what you need to do:
  • Click here to go to ecard homepage to browse ecard categories
  • Click on the card thumbnail you like to go to step 1.
  • Step 1 will let you view card full size with options allow you to add Image Effects (Java applet), change Skin background, change stamp icon, add music, poem...
  • If you would like to put this card to your favorites place, click Add to favorite button
  • If you want to choose another ecard, click More cards button
  • Click Continue Next Step button to personalize your card
  • Then enter the details asked for such as the recipient's name and email address and card message.
  • To see what your card will look like to your friend click the PREVIEW button. When you are happy with your card click the SEND NOW button to send the card to your friend.
How to pickup a card?
  • You will receive an e-mail stating that someone has sent you an ecard. In the e-mail message, there should be a web page link (URL) which you can click on to automatically receive your card.
